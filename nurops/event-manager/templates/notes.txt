- Event history endpoint: http://{{ .Release.Name }}.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc.cluster.local:80/events

To configure Alertmanager to send alerts to this service, add this to your Alertmanager config:

receivers:
  - name: 'event-manager'
    webhook_configs:
      - url: 'http://{{ .Release.Name }}.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc.cluster.local:80/events'
        send_resolved: true
